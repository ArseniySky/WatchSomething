About

Find a movie or tv show to watch. If you don't have any idea what to watch, you're at the right place. With this website you can easily:

  • Search for movies and tv shows by title, year, actor & genre.
  • List the top rated, latest, popular & upcoming movies.
  • List popular, top rated and currently On Air tv shows.
  • Add movies and tv shows to your watchlist or favorites list !
  • Get detailed informations about a movie or tv show.

This is a personal learning project, without any commercial / profitable intentions.
